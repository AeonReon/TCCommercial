Our Story

Local, Honest & Family-Run — Right Here in Ballymoney

TC Commercials isn't just a van dealership — it's a family business built on old-fashioned values. Based on the Kilraughts Road in Ballymoney, we've been helping local tradespeople, businesses and families find the right van at the right price for years.

We know that buying a van is a big decision, especially when it's the backbone of your livelihood. That's why every van we sell is fully serviced, multi-point checked and MOT-ready before it goes on our forecourt. No shortcuts, no surprises.

As well as van sales, we offer vehicle rentals through our Practical Car & Van Rental partnership, MOT preparation, full vehicle servicing, and flexible finance options — everything under one roof.
